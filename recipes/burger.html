Burger Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 cup chopped onion
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • Lettuce, tomato, cheese, and condiments for topping

Instructions:

Step 1: In a bowl, combine ground beef, breadcrumbs, egg, chopped onion, minced garlic, salt, and pepper. Mix until well combined.

Step 2: Divide the mixture into 4 equal portions and shape them into burger patties.

Step 3: Preheat your grill or stovetop pan over medium-high heat. Cook the burger patties for 4-5 minutes per side or until they reach your desired level of doneness.

Step 4: Toast the hamburger buns on the grill or in a toaster until golden brown.

Step 5: Assemble the burgers by placing the cooked patties on the buns. Add lettuce, tomato, cheese, and your favorite condiments on top.

Step 6: Serve immediately and enjoy your delicious homemade burgers!
