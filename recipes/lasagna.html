Lasagna Recipe

Description:

Indulge in the rich and savory flavors of this classic lasagna recipe. Layers of perfectly cooked noodles, flavorful meat sauce, and a cheesy, gooey goodness that will leave you craving for more.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 12 lasagna noodles, cooked and drained
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Italian seasoning for flavor

Steps:

  1. In a large skillet, brown the ground beef over medium heat. Add chopped onions and garlic, and cook until onions are translucent.
  2. Stir in crushed tomatoes, salt, pepper, and Italian seasoning. Simmer for 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  3. In a bowl, mix together ricotta cheese, Parmesan cheese, mozzarella cheese, and beaten egg.
  4. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  5. In a baking dish, layer cooked lasagna noodles, meat sauce, and cheese mixture. Repeat the layers, ending with a layer of cheese on top.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven for 30-35 minutes or until the cheese is bubbly and golden brown.
  7. Allow the lasagna to cool for a few minutes before slicing. Serve and enjoy your delicious homemade lasagna!
